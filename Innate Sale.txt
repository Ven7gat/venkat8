1.Find total quantity sold by each salesperson.
Ans:  select salesperson,count(quantity) from sale group by salesperson;

2.Find salespersons who sold more than 5 items in total.
Ans:  select salesperson,count(quantity) from sale group by salesperson having count(quantity)>5;

3.Find number of products sold by each salesperson.
Ans: select salesperson,count(quantity) from sale group by salesperson;

4.List regions where total sale amount exceeded $1000.
Ans: select region from sale group by region having sum(saleamount)>1000;

5.Show salespersons who made more than 2 sales transactions.
Ans: select salesperson from sale group by salesperson having count(quantity)>2;

6.Find total sale amount by product, and show only products that made over $500 in sales.
Ans: select product from sale group by product having sum(saleamount)>500;

7.Show the total quantity sold of each product in each region.
Ans:  select count(quantity) from sale group by region,product;

8.Find salespersons who sold more than 1 type of product.
Ans: select salesperson from sale group by salesperson having count(product)>1;

9.Find the average quantity per product sold per region, where the average is greater than 1.
Ans: select avg(quantity) from sale group by product,region having avg(quantity)>1;

10.Show salespersons whose total sale amount is between $500 and $1500.
Ans: select salesperson from sale where saleamount between 500 and 1500;

11.List top-performing products (more than 3 units sold in total).
Ans:  select product from sale group by product having sum(quantity)>3 ;

12.List salespersons who sold laptops.
Ans:  select salesperson from sale where product='laptop';

13.Find total sale amount per salesperson per region, only where it exceeds $500.
 Ans: select sum(saleamount) from sale group by salesperson,region having sum(saleamount)>500;

14.Find salespersons who sold at least 2 different products in a single region.
Ans: select salesperson from sale group by salesperson,region having count(distinct product) >1;

15.Find products that were sold in more than one region.
Ans: select product from sale group by product having count(distinct region)>1;

16.Show total number of sales per product and hide products with fewer than 2 sales.
Ans:  select product from sale group by product having sum(quantity)>2;

17.Find region-wise total quantity sold by each salesperson.
Ans:  select sum(quantity) from sale group by region,salesperson;

18.Find salespersons who made more than 3 transactions and total sales amount is over
$1000.sss
Ans: select salesperson from sale group by salesperson having sum(quantity)>3 and sum(saleamount)>1000;

19.Find average sale amount per region and show only regions with average above $200.
Ans:  select region from sale group by region having avg(saleamount)>200;

20.List regions and products where more than 3 units were sold.
Ans:  select region,product from sale group by region,product having sum(quantity)>3;
